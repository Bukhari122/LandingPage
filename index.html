Crew Poisitions

NavigatorThe navigator is responsible for charting the ship's course, using maps, stars, and the compass to guide the crew safely through treacherous waters.
HelmsmanThe helmsman steers the ship, keeping it on the navigator's chosen course. They handle the ship's wheel, adjusting for wind and current, and respond quickly during battles or stormy seas.
MusicianThe musician keeps the crew's spirits high during long voyages with shanties and tunes. They set the rhythm for rowing, entertain during mealtimes, and keep morale up even in the harshest conditions.
Ship-DocterThe ship doctor, often a skilled surgeon or herbalist, treats injuries and illnesses among the crew. They handle battle wounds, scurvy, and other ailments, using whatever supplies are available.
